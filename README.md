# Ground

Keep your feet on the ground. It's okay to live how you can afford.

The Problem:

Social media creates a bubble. We see our friends eating out daily, buying new clothes monthly, traveling constantly. What we don't realize is that this constant exposure subconciously affects our mood and makes us want to do the same—regardless of whether we can afford it.

The wealth gap is real. Not everyone can live that lifestyle, and **that's okay**. But instead of fighting the world, we can focus on being happy with what we have *right now*, while working toward a better future.

You only live once. Just once.

**The Solution**

Ground is a daily companion that reminds you it's okay to live within your means. It's not about accepting poverty—it's about accepting reality, staying grounded, and finding happiness despite circumstances.

Think of it like a voice that keeps your feet on the ground when social media tries to lift you into a world you can't afford.

**Features:**

**Daily Reminders**
Choose how many affirmations you want each day:
- **1 sentence** - Quick mindfulness
- **2 sentences** - A little more reflection
- **3 sentences** - Deep daily message

Personalized reminders that it's okay to be where you are right now.

**Journal** (Subscription)
Write freely each day. Your thoughts, your wins, your reality.
- Optional database saving (or keep it private locally)
- Track your mindset over time
- Premium feature to support the app's development

**Daily Streak**
Open the app every day and build your streak. Consistency compounds—just like your future wealth will.

**Calming Design**
A warm beige color scheme with thoughtful typography. Something that's easy on the eyes and pleasant to use. An app that doesn't drain your energy—it restores it.

**Target Audience**

- **Students & Young Professionals** managing limited budgets
- **Anyone** feeling the pressure of social media comparison
- **People** seeking financial peace of mind
- **Those** working toward their goals but needing grounding in the present

**RoadMap**

- Basic daily reminders
- Daily streak system
- Journal feature (premium)
- iOS version
- Smart notification timing
- Insights on mood & spending patterns
- Dark mode

**How it works**

The app is free with optional premium features:
- **Free**: Daily reminders + streak tracking
- **Premium**: Unlock journal feature & full database sync

This helps me (a college student) support the app's development while keeping it accessible to everyone who needs it.
